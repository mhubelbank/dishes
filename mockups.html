Cooking app — screen mockups

Fifteen screens for the personal cooking app, mobile breakpoint (390px). Directional, not pixel-perfect: hierarchy, copy tone, and behavior annotations are the spec; exact spacing is not. See requirements.md for full feature behavior and README.md for precedence rules.

Design voice: a quiet, attentive household member. Warm paper surfaces, one herb-green accent, serif reserved for personal-memory material (cook notes, past obsessions). No dashboards, no streaks.

Behavior. 9 · Push notification — garden event in cooking voice

Delivered by Home Assistant, written by the app. The critical UX rule from the addendum: it reads as a cooking decision, never device-speak. Tapping deep-links to Today with the caprese suggestion pre-selected — not to the Garden page.

Behavior. Copy template: [food] is ready — [dinner idea]? [supporting inventory fact]. Generated by one short LLM call per event, cacheable by template; never "Slot 4 status changed: ready".
